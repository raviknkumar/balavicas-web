Sai Krushna Charitable Trust came into existence with the direct command, blessings and instructions of Bhagavan Sri Sathya Sai Baba, to serve the underprivileged with love and provide them a life of purpose and honour. 

NEWS & EVENTS

Summer Camp

Chaitanya summer camp was held in April 2019, new skills like t-shirt painting, jewellery
making, algorithmic thinking, singing etc was taught to studnts and teachers alike. They say  "No more purchase of clothes
and accessories".

Pre primary

The new pre-primary section has started operating since June 2019. Our volunteers ensured that we have a bright room filled with colourful images painted on the walls. Do come and be part of the fun and learning.

Prize Distribution Function

For their outstanding performance in good behavior, academics, sports and cultural activities, students won numerous prizes in academic year 2018-19. The prize distribution function was held on 29th June, 2019

International Yoga Day

International Yoga Day was celebrated in School on 21st June 2019. All the teachers and students participated to build healthy bodies and a sound mind.

New Uniforms

Children look bright, smart and well groomed as they show off their new uniforms. Our school introduced new uniforms from academic year 2019-20. 

Parthi Yatra

The students and teachers of classes 6-7 of the Sai Krushna Vidya Mandir school were taken for the SSSVV annual meet to Puttaparthi. They also got the unique chance of leading the Mandir bhajans on 1st December, 2018.

Our Partners

We believe in the power of collaboration with different like-minded partners trying to solve similar issues.
